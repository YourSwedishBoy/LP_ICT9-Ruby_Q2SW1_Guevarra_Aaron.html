Favorite Scream Characters

# Character Name Image Description
1 Billy Loomis Billy Loomis is the boyfriend of Sidney Prescott. He tricked and played with Sidney. He tricked Sidney into thinking he wasn't the ghost face but in reality, he is the ghostface. His partner in crime is Stu Macher.
2 Stu Macher Stu Macher, the partner in crime of Billy Loomis, is also the ghost face. Stu and Billy together make a strong and formidable duo because they're both strong. They also have feats of lifting cars without much effort and chasing down Sidney while they were injured, keep in mind that Sidney was considered fast for some people.
3 Sidney Prescott Sidney Prescott was the girlfriend of Billy Loomis. I like her because unlike other protagonists in other horror movies who just run away, she tries to fight back even though her opponents are strong men, and there were two as well. She's smart compared to other horror movie characters whose goal is only to run away even though the killer has good agility and speed, they don't take advantage of what's around them unlike Sidney.
